About NicheCalc Tools

Free, accurate, and practical financial calculators for people who make things — small business owners, creators, sellers, and digital entrepreneurs.

Our Mission

Every day, millions of people start a side hustle, open an Etsy shop, launch a YouTube channel, or begin selling on Amazon. They're great at making things — but they often struggle to answer basic financial questions: How much am I actually earning per sale? What's my real profit margin? Am I pricing myself correctly?

We built NicheCalc to answer those questions in seconds. No spreadsheets. No guesswork. Just enter your numbers and see the truth — instantly.

What We Believe

  • Financial clarity should be free. You shouldn't need expensive software to understand your own numbers.
  • Accuracy matters. Every calculator is manually reviewed and updated when platforms change their fee structures.
  • Simple is better. A good calculator fits on one screen and gives you a clear answer without jargon.
  • Privacy is a feature. We don't ask you to sign up, and we don't sell your data.

How We Keep Calculators Accurate

Each calculator is built and maintained by reviewing official documentation from the platforms we cover. When Etsy updates its transaction fee percentage, when Amazon changes its fulfillment fee tiers, or when Google adjusts AdSense reporting — we update our calculators within days.

We also rely on community feedback from actual users. If you spot an error or a platform change we haven't caught, please let us know and we'll investigate and update promptly.

Our Editorial Process

  • Research. Review official platform fee schedules and developer documentation.
  • Build. Code the calculator logic in plain JavaScript — no hidden formulas.
  • Verify. Cross-check against actual invoices and seller dashboards.
  • Publish. Launch with real-world examples and FAQ sections.
  • Maintain. Review monthly; update within 48 hours when platforms change.
