How Your Dropshipping Profit Works

Four inputs. Five outputs. One clear picture of unit economics.

$

COGS

Product cost from supplier. Sourcing from AliExpress, Oberlo, or private suppliers. The lower, the better — but quality matters.

%

Margin

Net profit ÷ selling price × 100. Healthy dropshipping margin is 15–30%. Above 30% is a premium product winner.

🎯

CAC

Customer acquisition cost — total ad spend to get one paying customer. Rule: keep CAC below 33% of AOV (average order value).

✖

Break-Even

Fixed costs ÷ profit per order. Tells you how many orders before you're profitable. Use this to set daily ad budget targets.

FAQ

Top dropshipper pricing questions, answered.

What is a good profit margin for dropshipping?+

A healthy dropshipping profit margin is 15–30% of revenue. Top-performing stores target 25–45% margin by sourcing cheap and pricing at 2.5× to 4× COGS. Below 15% margin is risky — ad volatility and payment holds can wipe you out.

How do you calculate dropshipping profit?+

Dropshipping profit = Selling Price − COGS − Shipping Cost − Payment Processing (2.9% + $0.30) − Platform Fee (amortized) − Ad Spend per Sale (CAC). Use our free dropshipping profit calculator above to test multiple pricing scenarios in seconds.

How much should I mark up my dropshipping product?+

As a rule of thumb: mark up 2.5× to 4× your COGS. If a product costs $5, sell it for $15–$20. If it costs $15, price it at $39–$59. The higher the product cost, the lower your markup percentage. Test 3 price points to find where conversion × margin peaks.

What Shopify fees cut into my profit?+

Shopify charges: (1) Monthly subscription $29–$299+; (2) Credit card rates 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction (Basic plan); (3) Third-party payment gateway fees. Transaction fees eat 2–5% of every sale. Factor these into every product you price.

Is dropshipping still profitable in 2026?+

Yes. Dropshipping remains profitable in 2026 — but it's more competitive and requires a real brand strategy. 2026 winners focus on: (1) Niche-specific products; (2) Higher-ticket ($80–$200) items; (3) Post-purchase upsells; (4) Email/SMS retention; (5) TikTok/FB micro-influencer traffic instead of pure paid ads.

How do I reduce my customer acquisition cost (CAC)?+

Strategies: (1) Test 5+ ad creatives per campaign — creative beats targeting; (2) Improve offer (free shipping over $X, BOGO); (3) Add subscription / bundling to increase AOV; (4) Organic TikTok/Reels as free top-of-funnel; (5) Post-purchase upsells & email retargeting; (6) Kill losing ad sets fast (3-day kill rule).
