Frequently Asked Questions

Everything eBay sellers want to know about fees in 2026.

How much does eBay charge per sale in 2026?+

eBay charges a final value fee based on your category (typically 11.7% to 15.0%, capped at $750 for most categories), plus a $0.30 per-order fee. Store subscribers get discounted final value fees and free monthly listing credits. Managed Payments processing is 2.9% + $0.30 per domestic order.

What is the eBay final value fee in 2026?+

Final value fees vary by category in 2026: Fashion 15.0%, Collectibles 13.25%, Home & Garden 13.25%, Business & Industrial 12.9%, Electronics 12.9%, Motors Parts & Accessories 11.7%, Books/Movies/Music 13.5%. Store subscribers save 2–3.5% on final value fees depending on their tier.

What are eBay insertion fees?+

eBay gives 250 free fixed-price listings per month for non-store sellers. Additional listings cost $0.35 each. Store subscribers get 250 free listings (Basic) up to 100,000 (Anchor) monthly. Fixed-price listings stay active for 30 days and Good-'Til-Cancelled listings renew automatically.

How does eBay Managed Payments work?+

Managed Payments is eBay's built-in payment processing system, replacing PayPal for most sellers. Fees are typically 2.9% + $0.30 per domestic order. International payments and higher-value items may have different processing rates. Payouts happen to your linked bank account, usually within 2 business days.

What are eBay Promoted Listings fees?+

Promoted Listings Standard lets you choose an ad rate between 2% and 30% of the sale price. You only pay when a buyer clicks your promoted listing and makes a purchase within 30 days. Promoted Listings Advanced uses a cost-per-click (CPC) model with daily budget controls. Top sellers use 8–12% as a sweet spot for visibility vs. profit.

Is an eBay Store subscription worth it?+

If you list more than 250 items monthly, sell higher-value items, or want category-specific tools, a Store subscription typically pays for itself through lower final value fees and free listing credits. Tiers in 2026: Basic $4.95, Premium $74.95, Anchor $349.95, Enterprise $2,999.95. Use our calculator to compare with and without a store.

How do I calculate my eBay profit?+

Profit = Total Revenue − Final Value Fee − Insertion Fee − Managed Payments Fee − Promoted Listings Fee − Product Cost (COGS) − Shipping Cost. Our free eBay fee calculator above computes this instantly with adjustable category, store tier, and promoted listings settings.
