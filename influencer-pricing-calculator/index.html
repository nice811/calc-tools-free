FAQ

Top questions about influencer pricing answered.

How much should I charge for a sponsored Instagram post?+

Standard formula: $100 per 10,000 followers × engagement factor × content type. Nano influencers (1K–10K): $50–$300 per post. Micro (10K–100K): $200–$1,500. Mid-tier (100K–500K): $1,500–$10,000. Macro (500K–1M): $10,000–$30,000. Mega (1M+): $30,000–$100,000+. Rates double or triple for Reels and video. Add 25–50% for usage rights, whitelisting, or exclusivity clauses.

How much do Instagram influencers make per post?+

Average earnings by follower tier: Nano (1K–10K) — $50–$300; Micro (10K–100K) — $200–$1,500; Mid-tier (100K–500K) — $1,500–$10,000; Macro (500K–1M) — $10,000–$30,000; Mega (1M+) — $30,000–$100,000+. Top creators in high-value niches (finance, luxury, SaaS) can exceed these ranges by 2–5×. Stories and single links are typically priced at 30–50% of post rate; Reels at 150–250%. Monthly retainers discount 10–20% off per-post rate.

What is a good engagement rate on Instagram?+

A healthy engagement rate by tier: Nano 1K–10K = 5–10%; Micro 10K–100K = 3–7%; Mid-tier 100K–500K = 2–4%; Macro 500K–1M = 1.5–3%; Mega 1M+ = 1–2%. Below 1% is low and brands pay less. Above 8% is exceptional — you can charge a 20–50% premium over standard rate cards. Engagement rate is the single most important metric brands look at beyond follower count.

How do I calculate my influencer rate?+

Formula: (Followers ÷ 10,000) × $100 × Engagement factor × Content type multiplier × Niche multiplier. Engagement factor: above 5% engagement = 1.2–1.5×; below 2% engagement = 0.5–0.8×. Content multipliers: Static post = 1.0×; Carousel = 1.1×; Reel/video = 1.5–2.5×; Story set = 0.3–0.5×. Always add 25–50% extra if the brand wants usage rights, paid amplification (whitelisted ads), or exclusivity/non-compete periods.

How many followers do I need to make money on Instagram?+

You can make money on Instagram with as few as 1,000 followers. Nano influencers (1K–10K) often earn $50–$300 per sponsored post and have the highest engagement rates. Micro influencers (10K–100K) have the best income-to-effort ratio and can earn $500–$3,000/month with 2–4 posts per month. Full-time influencer income requires 50K+ engaged followers and diversified income streams (affiliate, own products, courses, UGC).

What do brands look for in influencers?+

Brands prioritize, in order: (1) Engagement rate — quality over quantity; (2) Niche fit — does your audience match their target customer?; (3) Content quality — aesthetic, captions, video production; (4) Follower demographics — country, age, gender; (5) Previous sponsored post results; (6) Brand safety and authenticity. A 10K micro influencer with 8% engagement in a specific niche often outperforms a 100K general lifestyle influencer with 2% engagement — and brands know it.
