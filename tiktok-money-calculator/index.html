Frequently Asked

Top questions about making money on TikTok.

How much does TikTok pay per 1000 views?+

TikTok's original Creator Fund pays approximately $0.02 to $0.04 per 1000 views. The newer TikTok Creativity Program (for 10K+ followers and 100K views in 30 days) pays roughly $0.20 to $3 per 1000 views — 10× higher. Actual earnings vary based on video duration, region, and ad demand.

How many followers do you need to make money on TikTok?+

You can start earning on TikTok with 1,000 followers (Live gifts), but meaningful income starts at 10,000 followers + 100K views in 30 days (Creativity Program). Mega creators with 1M+ followers earn $5,000–$50,000+ per month from brand deals alone.

How much do TikTokers make with 100K followers?+

A TikTok creator with 100K followers typically earns $500–$3,000/month from the Creativity Program, plus $1,000–$10,000 from brand deals and affiliate marketing. Total range: $1,500–$13,000/month depending on niche and consistency.

How do I calculate my TikTok earnings?+

TikTok earnings ≈ (Views ÷ 1000 × RPM) + (Followers × brand rate per thousand). Use our TikTok money calculator above to enter your followers, average views, and engagement rate for a realistic projection that includes all 4 major income streams.

Which TikTok niches pay the most?+

Top-paying TikTok niches by RPM and brand deal value: Finance & Investing (highest), Business & SaaS, Real Estate, Tech & Gadgets, Personal Development, Fitness, Beauty. Entertainment and meme accounts have the lowest ad RPM but can scale via merch/shoutouts.

How can I grow faster and earn more?+

Proven strategies: (1) Post 4–7 videos per week — consistency beats virality; (2) Pick one niche and stick to it (algorithmic + advertiser signal); (3) Hook viewers in 0–1 seconds — the TikTok hook is everything; (4) Turn comments into follow-up videos; (5) Diversify income beyond ad revenue (brands, affiliate, digital products).
